# ROFL App

*Runtime OFf-chain Logic (ROFL)* apps are applications running on Oasis nodes
inside a *Trusted Execution Environment (TEE)* that are managed through the
Oasis Sapphire blockchain.

ROFL introduces the following features:

- support for Docker-like containers or single-executable apps depending on your
  TCB demand and threat model
- integrity and auditability of your app including full history of updates
- uncensorable registration, management and deployment of your app on a
  permissionless pool of ROFL nodes including billing
- built-in Key Management Service (KMS) for storing your app secrets and
  secure derivation of keys within TEE
- integration with Oasis Sapphire that enables EVM-compatible smart contracts
  verification of the ROFL transaction origin

![ROFL diagram](images/rofl.svg)

These features allow you to implement and deploy provably secure decentralized
oracles, bridges, AI agents, game servers and more!
